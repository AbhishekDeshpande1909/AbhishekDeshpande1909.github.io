About Us

CRICSTATZ is an online system to manage cricket stats and results for domestic cricket bodies, leagues, associations and clubs. It keeps track of fixtures of upcoming matches,results of previous matches and leaderboard of the tournament. CRICSTATZ works on any internet-enabled device.
